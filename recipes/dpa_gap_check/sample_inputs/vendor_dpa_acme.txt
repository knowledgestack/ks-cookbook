DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM
========================
Effective Date: 2026-04-15
Controller: Customer (signing the Master Subscription Agreement)
Processor: Acme Cloud Services Inc., 100 Market St., San Francisco, CA

1. SCOPE AND ROLES
1.1 This DPA applies whenever Acme Processes Personal Data on behalf of
Customer in connection with the Services. Customer is the Controller;
Acme is the Processor. Where Customer is itself a Processor, Acme acts
as a sub-processor.

2. PROCESSING INSTRUCTIONS
2.1 Acme will Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from
Customer, including with regard to international transfers, unless
required by Union or Member State law.

3. SUB-PROCESSORS
3.1 Customer authorizes Acme to engage the sub-processors listed at
acme.com/subprocessors. Acme will provide notice of any new sub-processor
at least 30 days prior to authorizing the new sub-processor.
3.2 Customer may object to a new sub-processor on reasonable grounds. If
the parties cannot resolve the objection within 30 days, Customer may
terminate the affected Services with a pro-rata refund.

4. SECURITY MEASURES
4.1 Acme will implement and maintain appropriate technical and
organizational measures to protect Personal Data, including:
    (a) Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
    (b) Access controls based on least privilege; multi-factor
        authentication for administrative access.
    (c) Annual penetration testing and quarterly vulnerability scans.
    (d) Personnel training on data protection at hire and annually.

5. PERSONAL DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION
5.1 Acme will notify Customer without undue delay, and in no event later
than 48 hours after becoming aware of a Personal Data Breach affecting
Customer Personal Data.

6. DATA SUBJECT REQUESTS
6.1 Acme will, taking into account the nature of the Processing, assist
Customer by appropriate technical and organisational measures, insofar
as this is possible, for the fulfilment of Customer's obligation to
respond to requests for exercising the Data Subject's rights laid down
in Chapter III of the GDPR.

7. RETURN OR DELETION
7.1 At termination of the Services, Acme will, at Customer's choice,
return or delete all Personal Data unless retention is required by law.
Deletion is final after 30 days from termination.

8. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
8.1 The parties incorporate the Standard Contractual Clauses (Module 2,
Controller-to-Processor) for transfers from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland
to third countries.

9. AUDITS
9.1 Customer may audit Acme's compliance with this DPA at most once per
year on 30 days' written notice. Acme may satisfy this obligation by
providing its most recent SOC 2 Type II report.
