Zoning Compliance Check Request

Address: 2100 S Congress Ave, Austin, TX 78704
Current Zoning: GR (Community Commercial)
Proposed Use: Indoor entertainment venue with food service (escape room
concept with a small cafe serving beer and wine)

The applicant plans to lease 4,200 sq ft of ground-floor retail space in an
existing commercial building on South Congress Avenue. The space was previously
a retail clothing store. The proposed concept is an immersive escape room
entertainment venue with 4 themed rooms, a lobby/waiting area, and a small cafe
serving coffee, beer, and wine (no liquor).

Hours of operation: 10:00 AM - 11:00 PM Sunday-Thursday, 10:00 AM - 12:00 AM
Friday-Saturday.

The property is on a commercially-zoned corridor but is across the street
(approximately 80 feet) from SF-3 residential zoning to the east.

Questions for analysis:
1. Is "indoor entertainment" a permitted use in GR?
2. Does the beer/wine service require CS-1 zoning or can it operate under GR?
3. Do compatibility standards apply given the proximity to SF-3 zoning?
4. Are there any conditional use permit requirements?
5. What are the applicable height/FAR/setback limits for GR?
